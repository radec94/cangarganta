The house

Accommodation for 6 people in 3 bedrooms, with the possibility of installing two beds for babies. Heating and hot water through all the house.

Interior

House distributed on two floors: The ground floor is a large hall, a room for two persons with bathroom, living room with TV, exit to an inner courtyard a small bathroom and a laundry room with washer, washing machine , dryer and an iron. The first floor has a dining room for 8 people, two bedrooms, a full bathroom, games room and a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, refrigerator, freezer, oven, microwave, induction cooker and a fireplace.

History

A brief history about Centenys.

  • Catalan country house from the XVIIIth Century

    Situated in the little country village of Centenys, just 5Km from Banyoles.

  • CENTENS

    The ancient features of this village dates from 1001, with the spelling of CENTENS, later, in 1017 with CENTELS and, since the XIVth Century, with CENTENYS.

  • The name of CENTENS

    Registered between 1001 and 1172, means that its toponym comes from a Roman Village's name is CENTENYS.

  • Varis propietaris

    The 1001 is the first time we can find this word in any known documentation, and it seems to own to the monastery of Cuixà, according to the bull of pope Sergi IV "Villiam Centens Cum finibus et terminis Suis". Later, according to the bull of pope Benedict VIII in 1017, it consists in the possessions in the monastery of Banyoles.
